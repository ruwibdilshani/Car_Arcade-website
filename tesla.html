Descriptions

The Tesla Model S offers a sleek and minimalist design, with a spacious and comfortable interior that can accommodate up to five adults and two children with an optional third-row seat. It features a large touchscreen display in the center console that controls most vehicle functions, including navigation, media, and vehicle settings. The Model S is available with various battery options, offering different levels of performance and range, with the Long Range and Plaid versions being the most popular.
